Rules for Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock


Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock, often abbreviated as RPSLS, is a delightful expansion of the classic Rock Paper Scissors game. This unique twist was popularized by the TV series "The Big Bang Theory."


In the show, the character Sheldon Cooper, portrayed by Jim Parsons, introduced this entertaining variation as a means of settling disputes. Sheldon's quirky explanation and humorous approach to the game quickly captured the hearts of viewers.

The rules are as follows:


  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Rock crushes Lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Spock smashes Scissors
  • Scissors decapitates Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper
  • Paper disproves Spock
  • Spock vaporizes Rock
  • Rock crushes Scissors
